Ribs

My mom's famous ribs

These are my mom's ribs. I said they were famous, but to be honest, they're only famous at our house. However, they are pretty famous, because she's been preparing them since before COVID.

They always come out a little bit different, because, even though she has and uses this exact recipe, most of the time she eyeballs all of the ingredients. Sometimes they may end up tasting more peppery, other times they taste a bit sweeter, or spicier. But as different as they come out every time, one thing they all have in common, is their undeniable delicousness. Here's how she makes them.

Ingredients:

  • 3kg of pork ribs.
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar.
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin.
  • 2 tablespoons of salt.
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered garlic.
  • 1 tablespoon of pepper.
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered onion.
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered chili.
