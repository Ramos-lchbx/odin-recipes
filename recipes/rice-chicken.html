Chicken with rice

Another one from my mom: Chicken with rice

This recipe is another classic in our family. There's something truly special about this one, it's just a very comforting, yet simple, dish.

When this recipe is getting made, the whole house knows it, because the smell is unlike any other, and makes us hungry wherever we are. Here's how to make it.

Ingredients:

  • 5 chicken thighs.
  • 1 cup of rice.
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of chicken stock.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Pepper to taste.
  • Paprika to taste.
  • Dry parsley to taste.
  • 1/2 a cup of red onion.
  • 1 tablespoon of diced garlic.

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken thighs with all of the spices.
  2. Sear the chicken thighs with oil in a pan.
  3. Remove the chicken thighs from the pan.
  4. Add the red onion and garlic to the pan with oil.

In an oven-proof dish (a big one):

  1. Pre-heat oven at 200C
  2. Add the rice and chicken stock to the dish.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Wait for it to boil.
  5. Add the chicken and onion mix to the dish.
  6. Cover and bake at 200C for 35 minutes
  7. Serve.
